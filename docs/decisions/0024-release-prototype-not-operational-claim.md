# Decision 0024: release a research prototype, not an operational detector claim

Date: 2026-07-14

Continue model development and prepare a polished public release authored by Advaith Praveen (APRK), while preserving both negative continuous evaluations. A model may be called a functioning research prototype when it has reproducible inference, saved weights, leakage-controlled development metrics, and clear input/output behavior. It may not be called operationally reliable, flight-ready, or proven to detect unseen continuous Apollo events unless a future prospectively untouched evaluation supports that claim.
